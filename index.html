About Us

Our journey began with a personal experience. When me and my family, including three children, moved from India to the UK, I have faced significant challenges in finding a place to stay. With no accommodation readily available, I struggled to secure a home. My nephew, who was already in the UK, stepped in to help, ensuring they found suitable housing.

At Buildwells Innovation Ltd, we understand the importance of having a safe and comfortable home. Our goal is to make the process of finding accommodation easier and more accessible, ensuring that everyone has a place to call home without unnecessary stress.
